How It Works

Two layers.
One continuous picture.

Layer 01

Satellite Scan

Lupa ingests imagery from multiple satellite providers — Sentinel-2 for city-scale coverage, Planet Labs for daily revisit at sub-meter resolution, and commercial feeds for mission-critical cadence. Spectral indices — BSI, NDVI, NDWI — filter for anomalous terrain signatures. Gemini 2.5 Flash classifies each candidate tile with in-domain few-shot examples, achieving 92.9% precision out of the box.

Google Earth Engine Sentinel-2 Planet Labs Gemini 2.5 Flash Daily Revisit
Layer 02

Street Coverage

360° cameras driven weekly capture ground-truth street-level footage. AI processes every frame to verify satellite detections, track anomaly aging, and surface new issues invisible from orbit. Weekly temporal cadence enables trend analysis and SLA compliance reporting.
