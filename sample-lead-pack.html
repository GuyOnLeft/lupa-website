FL multi-county leads carry same-owner ownership across multiple FL counties (FL §196.031 — single primary residence rule). All cases pass our 24-month MLS-grade sale-date guardrail (verified via Redfin/MLS data) — the most common false-positive class in homestead audit work (recent ownership transfers where the new owner inherited the prior owner's exemption flag) is filtered out automatically.
